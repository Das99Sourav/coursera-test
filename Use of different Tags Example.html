How to sip the Kolkata style tea:

The Kolkata style tea is one of the best style of tea available across the country. The tea stalls are the lifeline of the people of Kolkata. The shops remain open 24/7 and 365 days of the year. The shops are located at every nooks and corners of the city. The shops are also a place of social gathering where people exchange there views. The small road side shops cater to all classes of people be it rich or poor. The tea in the streets of Kolkata is served in the earthen pots insted of cups. The earthen pots add a special flavour to the tea and provides it with an earthly aroma.

How to make the Kolkata style tea.

Ingredients requird:

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Tea
  • Ginger
  • Elaichi
  • Clove

How to make it:

  1. Bring half cup of water to boil in the stove.
  2. Add 5-6 table spoon of sugar to the boiling water.
  3. Crush one or two Cloves and Elichi in a crushur to open the pods.
  4. Crush an inch of ginger in crusher to bring out its extracts.
  5. Add the crushed spices in to the boiling water along with the crushed Ginger.
  6. Add a cup of full fat milk in to the boiling water.
  7. When the Milk and Water mixture comes to boil add 2-3 tea spoons of Tea in the mixture.
  8. The Tea is then boiled in the mixture for some time.
  9. Pass the mixture through strainer.
  10. Serve hot in earthen pots with biscuits as complimentary.
